For Donations

The free market runs on donations of goods and services from the community.

We will have donation locations and times posted for each instance, so please pay attention to flyers or social media posts! Often we have time before the event itself to sort through donations and organize them.

Our general rule is if you wouldn't give it to a friend, don't give it to us. For more specifics on our donation criteria, see our Giving Guide here!

Volunteering

Thanks for your interest! We are a totally volunteer-run operation, and we could always use more hands! Volunteers will be tasked with any number of duties, including:

  • Setup
  • Donation accepting/sorting
  • Services (e.g. mending, dependant on volunteer skills)
  • Breakdown

Volunteers get first pick!
